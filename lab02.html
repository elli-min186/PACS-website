What is PACS?

  • PACS is the pathway from theory to practical application
  • PACS aims to help bring your idea to reality
  • At PACS, we work on projects that impacts community with visible outcomes

What is our mission?

  • To bridge the gap between academia nad real world
  • To promote how technology applies to different aspects of life and workplace
  • To guide students throughout their personal projects

What events are we doing?

  • Workshops to apply knowledge from school
  • Networking events to gain insights from professionals in the industry
  • Fun social events to meet new people in every program
